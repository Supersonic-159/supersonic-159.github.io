Making Friends

Here are some tips for making friends in high school:

Be yourself: Authenticity attracts genuine friendships.

Join clubs or activities: Find groups that interest you to meet like-minded people.

Start conversations: Ask open-ended questions to get to know others.

Be a good listener: Show interest in what others have to say.

Be positive: A friendly attitude can draw people to you.

Be patient: Building friendships takes time, so don't rush it.

Remember, making friends takes time and effort, so be patient and keep trying!
