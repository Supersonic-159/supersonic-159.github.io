Behaviour

In high school, you will encounter a variety of people and situations. It's important to be respectful and considerate towards others. Here are some tips on how to behave in high school:


- Be polite and respectful to your teachers and classmates.

- Listen actively when someone is speaking to you.

- Avoid gossiping or spreading rumors about others.

- Be open-minded and accepting of different opinions and backgrounds.

- Participate in class discussions and activities.

- Help others when you can, whether it's with schoolwork or personal issues.

- By following these guidelines, you can create a positive and supportive environment for yourself and your peers.

- Remember, high school is a time for growth and learning, so make the most of it!


If you have any questions or need advice, feel free to reach out to your teachers or school counselors.

They are there to help you succeed and navigate through high school.
